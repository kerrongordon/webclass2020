ZONES

The Grenada Cadet Corps is divided into a Headquarter and three (3) zones across the island; as a result, the corps is represented in the six parishes of Grenada by any one or more zones.

ZONE A

Zone Commander Captain Marta Budd

Zone A cadets consist of cadets from the northern part of the island; St.Mark's, St.Patrick's and St.Andrew's.

There are five units in Zone A. The units are;

  1. Grenada SDA Comprehensive School - Warrant Officer Class 2 Alrick Andrew
  2. Grenville Secondary School - Warrant Officer Class 2 Neil Noel
  3. Mac Donald College - Under Officer Hamlet
  4. St.Andrew's Anglican Secondary School - Warrant Officer Class 2 Neil Noel
  5. St.Mark's Secondary School - Captain Marta Budd

ZONE B

Zone Commander Captain Ralph James

Zone B cadets consist of cadets from St.Geroge's.

There are four units in Zone B. The units are;

  1. Boca Secondary School - Adult Sargent Randy Benjamin
  2. Happy Hill Secondary School - Adult Sargent Neckles
  3. Presentation Brothers College - Warrant Officer Class 2 Louvelle Joseph
  4. Wesley College - Under Officer Keith Blackman
