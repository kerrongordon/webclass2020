About the Grenada Cadet Corps

The Grenada Cadet Corps is a youth movement with a difference. The difference being that it is a disciplined organization with a rank structure. Working within this environment the youths are given a feeling of stability and purpose. The training is based upon millitary skills but the aim is not to produce a highly trained soldier. The aim of all the training is carefully planned so that it appeals in the first place as a challenge; secondly it is designed to develop the powers of leadership and qualities of character valuable alike to the civillian and soldier.
