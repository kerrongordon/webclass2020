Am I Eligible?

Both secondary school students and adults may applyto join the CADET CORPS. Students can apply to join a cadet unit at another school if no unit is established at the school they are attending.

As a secondary school student, you are eligible to join as a cadet if you are:

  • A citizen or legal resident of Grenada, Carriacou & Petite Martinique.
  • Enrolled in a government, government-assisted, or private school in Grenada, Carriacou & Petite Martinique.
  • Between 11 and 16 years of age

As an adult, you are eligible to joing as a Non-Commissioned Officer if you are:

  • A citizen or legal resident of Grenada, Carriacou & Petite Martinique.
  • Have successfully completed your secondary education, with passes in English and at least two other subjects.
  • Are 21 years of age or older.
  • Recommended by two citizens of Grenada, Carriacou & Petite Martinique.

How do i apply?

if you are a secondary school student, please follow these steps to apply:

  1. Obtain a student application form from the Cadet Unit Commander incharge of your school.
  2. Complete the application form and have it signed by your parent or guardian and the Principal of your school.
  3. if you are applying to join Cadet at another school, have the form signed by the principal of that school.
  4. Return your completed and signed form to the Cadet Unit Commanding officer on the designed day.

If you are an adult, please follow these steps to apply:

  1. Obtain an adult application form by following the link below. You may also obtain a form from the Grenada Cadet Corps Office.
  2. Complete the application form and sign it
  3. Return your completed and signed form at the Grenada Cadet Corps Office
