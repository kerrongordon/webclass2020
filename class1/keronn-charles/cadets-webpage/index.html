The Grenada Cadet Corps is the youngest existing organisation for young people in Grenada, Carriacou & Petite Martinique. Training in the Cadet Corps is based on the British Army Proficiency Certificate Four Star Syllabus and emphasize military training and discipline with the objecitve of transforming young people into productive, caring citizens of Grenada, Carriacou & Petite Martinique. This type of training also makes the Cadet Corps an invaluable source for recruits for the protective services. Cadet Corps Units are based in foreteen (14) Secondary Schools throughout the island Grenada.
