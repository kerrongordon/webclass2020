What it takes to be Great

Inorder to be a great player or one of the greatest to ever play the game of basketball, the approach must be different from most players. Its takes really hard work, high level understanding of the game and a drive to win by constantly aiming to improve daily. Kid you not some players do have all the criteria to win championships but has yet to or never completed that task. Over every decade a legend is born, while some hide in the shadows of others.
