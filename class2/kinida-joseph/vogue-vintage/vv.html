About Us

VintageVogue- Providing all your vintage fashion needs for the 21st century.

VintageVogue is a virtual and physical store that has been in existence since 2016. Its is to provide modern vintage fashion to the public and encourage vitage self-expression. VintageVogue has maintained localized sites and stores in the United Kingdom, United States and Denmark. The company was headquartered St.George, Grenada and was founded by Kinida Joseph.
